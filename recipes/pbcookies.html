Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies

Description

This is my favorite cookie recipe. I can eat them anytime and they are super simple to make

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cup peanut Butter
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup shortening
  • 3-3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tsp vanilla
  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1-1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups rolled oats

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients together in large bowl
  2. Roll dough into ball and use fork to make a criss cross pattern
  3. bake at 350 degrees for approximately 14 minutes
